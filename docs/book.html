Preview Spreads

Three illustrated spreads from the manuscript.

Pages 4–5 — Into the Ring
Birch spotted it first — a circle of mushrooms, glowing at the edges.
"A fairy ring!" Fern whispered.
Birch grabbed her hand. "Only one way to find out."
They stepped inside — and the forest dropped away.
Theme: Wonder, courage, the call to adventure • Science: Fairy rings are real — fungi growing outward in circles
Pages 6–7 — Down to Sillium
The world grew BIGGER.
Grass became trees. Pebbles became boulders.
They fell through swirling golden light — and landed in a cavern of glowing blue threads.
A warm voice echoed: "Welcome to the network!"
Theme: Transformation, scale, arriving somewhere extraordinary • Science: Most of a fungus is underground
Pages 8–9 — The Guide
"I'm Fun-Gus." He traced a glowing thread with his finger. "Every one of these is a conversation. Trees talking to each other. Sharing food. Sending warnings."

He paused. "But today we have a problem."

He pointed down a fading thread. "A little seedling is losing its connection. Want to help me find it?"
Theme: Cooperation, purpose, a mission to help • Science: Mycorrhizal networks connect 90% of plants

Series Potential

Five books planned, each introducing new characters and science concepts.

Book 2: The Thirsty Forest

Birch and Fern return during a drought. Meet Morel the wanderer and the Recyclers. Learn about decomposition and water sharing.

Book 3: The Bright One

Meet Russula, who learns that cracks don't make you broken. Meet Physa the engineer. Learn about fragility, identity, and problem-solving.

Book 4: The Impossible Path

Meet Tardi the indestructible. Venture to the network's dangerous edges. Learn about extremophiles and the limits of life.

Book 5: The Old Enemy

Cordyceps arrives. The whole network must work together. Learn about parasitism vs. cooperation.
