## 1. COMIC: "THE THIRSTY TREE" (10 pages, 24 panels)

**Comic page ratio:** Standard US comic is 6.625" x 10.25" (roughly 2:3 portrait).

| Panel Type | Ratio | Sora 2 Dimensions | Use |

| STANDARD (quarter-page) | 4:3 | 1440x1080 | Dialogue, medium shots |
| CLOSE-UP | 1:1 | 1080x1080 | Faces, details, emotion |
| TALL (half-page vertical) | 3:4 | 1080x1440 | Action, reveals |
| FULL SPLASH (entire page) | 2:3 | 1080x1620 | Hero moments |
| SPLIT (cross-section) | 3:4 | 1080x1440 | Above/below ground |
