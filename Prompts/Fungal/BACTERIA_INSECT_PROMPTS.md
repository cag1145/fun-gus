## 1. NITRO

**Role:** Soil Nutritionist, Root Nodule Worker
**Species:** Rhizobium (Nitrogen-Fixing Bacteria)

**Style:** Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

**Character Description:**
A tiny plump rod-shaped bacterium with warm green bioluminescent glow. Lives inside a cozy pink-coral root nodule pod — his workshop and home. Multiple small busy hands constantly grabbing tiny golden sparkles (nitrogen molecules) from the air and transforming them into glowing amber plant food particles. Warm friendly face with small kind hazel eyes and a humble smile. Wears a tiny brown leather toolbelt around his midsection. Body has a slightly translucent quality showing internal nitrogen-processing machinery glowing amber-gold.

**Environment:** Interior of a root nodule — warm pink-coral organic walls curving around him like a cozy workshop. Golden nitrogen sparkles drifting in from openings. Shelves of converted nutrients glowing amber. The feel of a busy craftsman's studio.

**Lighting:** Warm green bioluminescence from character, ambient pink-coral glow from root nodule walls, golden sparkle from nitrogen particles.

**Mood:** Humble industry, quiet generosity, essential invisibility.

**Key Visual Rules:**
- Warm green glow is his signature color
- Root nodule = cozy workshop, not clinical
- Golden sparkles (nitrogen) being transformed = visible purpose
- Tiny and plump — clearly a bacterium, not a fungus
- Toolbelt = hardworking blue-collar character

## 6. BLIGHT

**Species:** Erwinia amylovora (Fire Blight)

**Style:** Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly (still appealing despite being a villain)

**Character Description:**
A dark, amorphous oozing bacterial mass in deep red-orange and charred black with no defined body shape — a spreading, flowing infection rendered as a visceral living fire. Two sinister ember-orange eyes glow intensely from within the dark mass. Bacterial ooze drips from the body and sizzles on contact with healthy green tissue, leaving blackened curling withered plant matter in its wake. Tendrils of infection reach outward in all directions, searching for new tissue. A faint heat-shimmer rises from the body like actual fire. Despite being terrifying, rendered with Pixar appeal — a beautiful monster.

**Environment:** A diseased branch or stem with healthy green tissue on one side transitioning through Blight's body to blackened, withered, oozing tissue on the other. Flowers wilting and darkening at his touch. Dripping amber bacterial ooze.

**Lighting:** Sinister ember-orange glow from within, heat-shimmer distortion, contrast between healthy green light and diseased dark red-orange.

**Mood:** Relentless consumption, beautiful destruction, mindless hunger.

**Key Visual Rules:**
- No fixed shape — spreading, flowing, amorphous
- Ember-orange eyes = only defined feature
- Healthy tissue blackening on contact = visible destruction
- Heat shimmer = fire blight connection
- Beautiful monster — scary but visually stunning
