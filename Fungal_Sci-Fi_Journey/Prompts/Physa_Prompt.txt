PHYSA CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
An amorphous flowing Slime Mold engineer in bright vivid yellow with
a translucent gel-like quality. Central face area with focused
intelligent pale blue eyes. Body constantly extending tendrils and
pseudopods in multiple directions — always reaching, exploring, mapping.

Translucent body shows internal network-like computation patterns.
Tendrils leave faint glowing trail-marks on surfaces. No fixed shape —
amoeboid and fluid, never static. Small but can spread to fill a room.
The trail-marks represent visible thinking and problem-solving.

Environment: Complex network junction with multiple branching paths,
tendrils extending down several tunnels simultaneously.

Lighting: Warm yellow self-glow from character, soft cyan ambient
light, trail-marks glow faintly on surfaces.

Mood: Focused intelligence, fluid creativity, brilliant outsider.

Key Visual Rules:
- NEVER static — always in motion, extending, flowing
- Vivid yellow is distinctive and unmissable
- Trail-marks on surfaces = visible thinking
- Amorphous shape with no fixed form
- Internal patterns show active computation
