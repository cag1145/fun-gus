INKY CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A tall elegant Ink Cap mushroom scribe. Shaggy cylindrical white cap
with curling scale-like edges. Bottom edges of cap dissolving into
dripping black-purple ink — a beautiful gradient from white to flowing
dark ink like watercolor bleeding. Slim pale stem-body with ink-stained
dark hands. Thoughtful dark eyes with purple undertones.

Calm composed expression. Carries a small worn bark satchel. Black-
purple calligraphic ink marks appear on nearby surfaces, beautiful
and deliberate. The dissolving cap is rendered as gorgeous, not
gruesome — an act of beautiful sacrifice.

Environment: Quiet SILLIUM alcove with walls covered in beautiful
ink-mark records and drawings, a natural writing desk of smooth stone.

Lighting: Soft warm ambient light, ink marks catch light with purple
iridescence, library-like atmosphere.

Mood: Thoughtful intensity, quiet purpose, beauty in sacrifice.

Key Visual Rules:
- Cap dissolving into ink is the KEY visual — beautiful not gruesome
- Ink stains on hands and surrounding surfaces
- White-to-black gradient on cap is signature look
- Calm composed posture always
- Purple-black color palette unique to Inky
