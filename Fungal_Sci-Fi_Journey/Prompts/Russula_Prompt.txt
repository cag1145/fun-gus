RUSSULA CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A striking Russula mushroom — the popular one. Perfectly round smooth
glossy scarlet-red cap like a lacquered jewel, vivid against earth
tones. Pristine white slender elegant stem-body. Bright expressive
green eyes with long lashes. Poised posture always at the best angle.

Hairline cracks in the cap she tries to hide, with a slight chip on
one edge. Stem elegant but fragile-looking. Golden-amber veins faintly
visible beneath the red cap surface, hinting at untapped potential.
Her beauty is real but so is her brittleness.

Environment: SILLIUM hub, positioned slightly apart from others in
the most flattering light available.

Lighting: Soft spotlight-like illumination, warm but slightly
artificial compared to other characters' natural glow.

Mood: Confident surface, hidden vulnerability, beautiful but breakable.

Key Visual Rules:
- RED is vivid and unmissable — her signature color
- Hairline cracks = the truth she hides
- Perfect posture = constant performance
- Fragile stem contrasts with bold cap
- Golden veins beneath surface = untapped potential
