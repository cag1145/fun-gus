PUFF CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A large perfectly round white-cream Giant Puffball like a giant
marshmallow with a face. Smooth slightly powdery surface. Enormous
bright blue eyes perpetually wide with excitement. Big goofy grin
with rosy cheeks. Stubby tiny arms and legs comically too small
for the massive round body.

Faint golden spore-powder dusting across surface. Small cracks in
the surface emit wisps of golden spore-clouds. Shown in a mid-burst
pose with a golden-amber spore cloud billowing dramatically from the
top of the body.

Environment: SILLIUM tunnel with other characters ducking or laughing
as golden spore-dust settles on everything and everyone nearby.

Lighting: Warm golden glow radiating from spore clouds, ambient
light catching floating spore particles.

Mood: Explosive joy, uncontainable excitement, pure comedy.

Key Visual Rules:
- SO round — roundness is everything
- Spore bursts are always golden-amber
- Eyes always wide and excited, never calm
- Surface cracks show emotional pressure building
- Other characters nearby covered in spore-dust
