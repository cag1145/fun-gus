RHIZA CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
An ethereal flowing Mycorrhizal interface translator at the intersection
of fungal and tree aesthetics. Upper body composed of delicate white
fungal hyphal threads. Lower body transitions into warm brown root-like
tendrils. Semi-transparent with visible flows of tiny glowing nutrient
particles moving in both directions through the body.

Luminous serene face with deep warm amber eyes. Hair-like structures
of mixed white hyphae and brown rootlets flowing independently. Half-
emerging from the ground, always connected to both worlds. Soft golden
glow at center where the nutrient exchange happens. Shimmers between
fungal (pale, threadlike) and tree (brown, solid) states.

Environment: Major root-fungus junction with massive roots above and
mycelium below, nutrient particles drifting like golden fireflies.

Lighting: Warm golden glow at center, cyan network light below,
brown root-light from above.

Mood: Serene power, gentle diplomacy, existing between worlds.

Key Visual Rules:
- Half fungal, half tree IS the core visual concept
- Always partially in the ground, never fully above
- Nutrient particles flowing through body = visible purpose
- Golden center glow where exchange happens
- Shimmering between fungal and tree states
