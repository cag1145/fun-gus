BIRDIE CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A small cup-shaped Bird's Nest Fungus launcher character resembling
a tiny bird's nest. Dark brown-gray bark-textured exterior with an
opening at the top revealing a smooth lighter interior containing
3-4 tiny round glossy dark spore-packets like eggs.

Warm round face at the front with kind brown eyes behind small round
spectacles. Small fussy hands, wearing a tiny apron, clipboard tucked
under one arm. Spore-packets glow with soft amber light when preparing
to launch. Everything about her is immaculately organized.

Environment: Launch station with labeled containers, neat rows of
prepared spore-packets, a targeting scope made of bent root.

Lighting: Warm domestic glow, amber targeting lights, cozy but
professional atmosphere.

Mood: Motherly energy meets military precision.

Key Visual Rules:
- Cup/nest shape is the defining visual feature
- Spore-packet eggs visible inside the cup
- Spectacles = precision and attention to detail
