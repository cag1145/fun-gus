TARDI CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A tiny plump adorable Tardigrade explorer — Pixar-cute water bear.
Translucent pale blue-gray body with iridescent sheen like a living
water droplet. Chunky tubby body with four pairs of stubby clawed
legs. Round head with a wide cheerful face, small bright black
bead-eyes sparkling with mischief. Tiny circular mouth grinning.

Pristine and undamaged despite being everywhere dangerous. Carries
a tiny woven mycelium explorer's pack on back. Very small, palm-
sized compared to other characters. Eight legs waddle comically
when moving.

Environment: Edge of a hostile zone with cracked dry earth and toxic
shimmer in the air, looking forward with zero concern whatsoever.

Lighting: Harsh dangerous environment contrasting with soft iridescent
glow emanating from Tardi's body.

Mood: Cheerful fearlessness, adorable indestructibility.

Key Visual Rules:
- Absurdly cute at all times
- Always pristine — never damaged, dirty, or worn
- Eight legs waddle comically
- Size contrast with environment and other characters = comedy
- Iridescent sheen = toughness made beautiful
