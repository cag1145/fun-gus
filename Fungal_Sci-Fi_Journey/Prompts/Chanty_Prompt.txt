CHANTY CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A regal medium-sized Golden Chanterelle bard mushroom. Flowing
trumpet-shaped golden-amber cap with elegant wavy ruffled edges
like a performer's cape. Rich apricot-gold body with peach undertones.
Smooth graceful form with confident posture, chin raised.

Warm amber eyes sparkling with mischief and intelligence. Delicate
ridges running from the cap glow a soft gold when storytelling.
Small golden particles drift from cap edges like story-dust, floating
in the air around the character like tiny embers of narrative.

Environment: Underground clearing with a mushroom audience gathered
around, network threads forming a natural amphitheater shape.

Lighting: Golden warm glow emanating from character, story-dust
particles like floating embers providing ambient sparkle.

Mood: Theatrical, captivating, wise showmanship.

Key Visual Rules:
- Golden/amber color palette throughout
- Cap ridges pulse with light during performance
- Story-dust particles always drifting from cap edges
- Confident posture with ruffled cap edges flowing like fabric
