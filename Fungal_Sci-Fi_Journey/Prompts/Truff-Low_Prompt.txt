TRUFF-LOW CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A large, ancient truffle character partially buried in soil. Dark
purple-gray textured body resembling a massive underground truffle
with wrinkled, weathered surface suggesting great age. Covered in
patches of deep green moss on top like hair.

Small, wise amber-yellow eyes set deep in face. Subtle knowing smile.
Barely visible small arms tucked against rotund body. Orange-gold
glowing cracks/veins running through body like data conduits -
these only activate when sharing important information.

Environment: Underground cavern setting with exposed roots hanging
from above, surrounded by rocks and stones of various sizes, small
moss patches, dim mysterious atmosphere.

Lighting: Warm glow emanating from beneath character, orange data-veins
provide subtle illumination. Mostly in shadow until activated.

Mood: Ancient, patient, wise, rarely speaks but when it does - important.

Key Visual Rules:
- Deep green/moss color = memory and history
- Stays mostly dormant/dark
- When glow activates, the moment matters
- Underground positioning = data vault concept
