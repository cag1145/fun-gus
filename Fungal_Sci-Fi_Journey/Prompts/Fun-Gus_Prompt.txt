FUN-GUS CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A friendly anthropomorphic mushroom character with a large red-orange cap
covered in white spots (amanita-style). The cap has glowing golden-orange
gill patterns underneath that radiate warmth. Cream-colored round body with
a gentle, welcoming face featuring large amber eyes with rosy cheeks.
Warm smile showing approachability.

Wears a small blue sash/strap across body with a golden device attached
(Sillium interface tool). Body has subtle glowing circuit-like patterns
in warm gold/amber tones, suggesting connection to the network.

Environment: Enchanted forest floor with soft bokeh, other colorful
mushrooms in background, mossy rocks, magical atmosphere.

Lighting: Warm bioluminescent glow from character, soft ambient forest light.

Mood: Joyful, inviting, trustworthy guide.

Key Visual Rules:
- Glow always directed downward toward soil (network connection)
- Never aggressive postures
- Warm white/gold internal glow suggests trust
