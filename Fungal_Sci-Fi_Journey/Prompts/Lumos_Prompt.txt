LUMOS CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A slender, delicate bioluminescent Mycena scout mushroom. Translucent
bell-shaped cap glowing blue-green. Silvery-white body with a nervous

Translucent body shows a faint cyan network of threads inside like
fiber optics. Thin graceful arms with small hands that leave trails
of soft light. Cap shifts from pale silver to vivid cyan-teal when
emotional. Overall impression of a small brave light in a vast dark.

Environment: Dark underground tunnel at the network edges, his glow
illuminating ancient dormant mycelium threads stretching into darkness.

Lighting: Character IS the light source — bioluminescent cyan-teal glow
casting soft illumination on surrounding tunnel walls and threads.

Mood: Brave but nervous, determined, small light in big dark.

Key Visual Rules:
- Glow intensifies with emotion
- Translucent body shows internal network connections
- Cyan/teal color language throughout
