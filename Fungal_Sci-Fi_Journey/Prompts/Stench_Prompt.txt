STENCH CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A tall unusual Stinkhorn character with a netted/latticed head structure
in olive-green and brown tones — open and airy like a natural lantern.
Thick sturdy white spongy stalk-body. Kind but guarded brown eyes
visible within the lattice framework.

Faint green-brown misty aura around the character (visualized scent,
NOT gross — gentle and atmospheric). Tiny friendly flies and beetles
perched on the lattice like loyal pets. Dignified posture despite
emotional guardedness. A misunderstood hero.

Environment: His own network section, lush with insect life but empty
of other fungi, beautiful in its own unique way.

Lighting: Warm amber light filtering through lattice creating dappled
patterns on surroundings, soft green ambient glow.

Mood: Dignified loneliness, quiet kindness, waiting to be seen.

Key Visual Rules:
- Lattice/net head structure is the defining feature
- Scent visualized as gentle misty aura, never gross
- Insect friends always present perched on lattice
- Kind eyes tell the real story behind guardedness
- Isolated but beautiful environment surrounds him
