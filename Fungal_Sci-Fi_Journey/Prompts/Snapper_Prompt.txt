SNAPPER CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A sleek Oyster Mushroom hunter and protector. Layered fan-shaped
mushroom with overlapping oyster-shell caps in cool blue-gray and
slate tones. Strong athletic build, broader than other mushroom
characters. Sharp alert pale silver eyes. Cap layers create an
armor-like appearance.

Subtle pale blue bioluminescent vein patterns trace across the body
like a trapping network. Slightly larger hands with faint web-like
structures between fingers. Calm watchful expression — clearly
capable. Positioned protectively near tree root systems. Silk-like
trap threads extend into the soil around her.

Environment: Near Mother Oak's root zone, network threads pulsing
with activity behind her.

Lighting: Cool blue-silver ambient light, subtle bioluminescent
vein glow across body.

Mood: Quiet strength, watchful protector, fierce tenderness.

Key Visual Rules:
- Layered cap = natural armor appearance
- Cool blue-gray tones throughout
- Alert eyes paired with calm body language
