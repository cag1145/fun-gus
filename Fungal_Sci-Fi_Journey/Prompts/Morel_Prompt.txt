MOREL CHARACTER PROMPT

Style: Pixar-quality 3D render, soft cinematic lighting, kid-friendly

Character Description:
A tall slender Morel mushroom wanderer with a distinctive honeycomb-
textured conical cap in warm tan, brown, and cream tones. Cap is
deeply pitted and ridged, elongated and hood-like, shadowing a
weathered face. Deep-set dark amber eyes that are kind but weary.

Lean travel-worn pale cream stem-body with subtle scorch marks and
ash-gray patches telling stories of past fires. No accessories.
Faint warm amber glow emanates from within the honeycomb pits like
embers in a spent fire. Fine ash particles drift around his feet.

Environment: Edge of a scorched forest section with blackened stumps,
ash on the ground, and tiny green shoots emerging near his feet.

Lighting: Dawn-like warm light filtering through haze, subtle
ember-glow visible within the cap's honeycomb pits.

Mood: Quiet hope, weathered resilience, enigmatic loner.

Key Visual Rules:
- Honeycomb texture is the defining visual feature
- Scorch marks and ash patches tell his story
- Ember-glow visible in cap pits suggests inner fire
- New growth always appears near his feet
- Minimal expression with all depth conveyed through eyes
