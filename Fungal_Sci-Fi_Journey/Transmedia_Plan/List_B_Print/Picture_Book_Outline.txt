PICTURE BOOK: MY FUN-GAL ADVENTURE
===================================
Format: 24-32 pages, large illustrations, minimal text
Audience: Ages 4-6 (read-aloud), 6-8 (independent)
Status: Concept Phase

SPECIFICATIONS
--------------
- Trim size: TBD (standard picture book ~8x10 or 10x10)
- Page count: 32 pages (including front/back matter)
- Text per spread: 1-3 sentences
- Illustration style: Match existing character art (Pixar-quality 3D render)

PAGE-BY-PAGE OUTLINE
--------------------

FRONT MATTER
- Page 1: Half-title
- Page 2-3: Title spread (girls entering forest)

THE STORY

Pages 4-5: THE WALK
- Girls and teacher walking through forest
- Text: "Maya and Fern loved exploring the forest with Ms. Meadows."
- Visual: Sunny forest path, teacher pointing at something

Pages 6-7: THE DISCOVERY
- One girl spots something glowing
- Text: "What's that?" asked Maya. Something was glowing between the trees."
- Visual: Girl pointing, soft glow visible in distance

Pages 8-9: THE RING
- They find the fairy ring
- Text: "A circle of mushrooms. And they were glowing."
- Visual: Beautiful fairy ring, cyan/amber light

Pages 10-11: THE STEP
- They step inside together
- Text: "Should we?" "Together."
- Visual: Three figures stepping into ring, sparkles beginning

Pages 12-13: THE SHRINKING
- World grows huge around them
- Text: "The world grew bigger... and bigger... and BIGGER."
- Visual: Dynamic shrinking sequence, massive mushrooms rising

Pages 14-15: UNDERGROUND
- They land in Sillium cavern
- Text: "They were underground. And it was beautiful."
- Visual: Vast glowing cavern, threads everywhere

Pages 16-17: MEETING FUN-GUS
- Fun-Gus appears
- Text: "Hello, little travelers! I'm Fun-Gus. Welcome to Sillium!"
- Visual: Fun-Gus glowing warmly, friendly pose

Pages 18-19: THE NETWORK
- Fun-Gus explains what they're seeing
- Text: "These threads connect every tree in the forest. We help them talk to each other."
- Visual: Fun-Gus gesturing at glowing network, girls amazed

Pages 20-21: MEETING SPORA
- Spora floats in, nervous
- Text: "This is Spora. She's learning to send her very first message."
- Visual: Tiny Spora with flickering glow

Pages 22-23: ENCOURAGEMENT
- Girls encourage Spora
- Text: "You can do it, Spora!"
- Visual: Girls cheering, Spora concentrating

Pages 24-25: SUCCESS
- Spora's glow stabilizes, message sent
- Text: "She did it! Her light flew through the network."
- Visual: Triumphant Spora, pulse racing through threads

Pages 26-27: TIME TO GO
- Fun-Gus guides them back
- Text: "Fun-Gus led them back to the ring. 'Visit anytime,' he said."
- Visual: Walking toward glowing ring in distance

Pages 28-29: RETURN
- Back to normal size
- Text: "The forest looked the same. But now they knew its secret."
- Visual: Girls and teacher, normal size, looking at forest differently

Pages 30-31: THE SECRET
- Closing image
- Text: "Beneath every forest is a network. And someone is always listening."
- Visual: Wide shot of forest, subtle glow visible at roots

BACK MATTER
- Page 32: "The Real Science" - brief facts about mycelium for parents

THEMES
------
- Curiosity leads to wonder
- Hidden worlds exist beneath the obvious
- Everyone can contribute (Spora's success)
- Nature is connected
