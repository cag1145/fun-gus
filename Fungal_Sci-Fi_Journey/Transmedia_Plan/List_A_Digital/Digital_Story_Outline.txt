DIGITAL STORY: MY FUN-GAL ADVENTURE
=====================================
Format: Illustrated narrative video (2-4 minutes)
Status: Concept Phase

SYNOPSIS
--------
Two curious girls on a nature walk with their teacher discover a glowing
mushroom ring. When they step inside, they shrink into the Sillium network
and meet Fun-Gus, who shows them how trees "talk" to each other underground.
They witness Spora learning to send her first message and return to the
surface seeing the forest differently.

KEY CONCEPTS TO INTRODUCE
-------------------------
- Mycelium as underground network
- Trees and fungi as partners
- Messages traveling through roots
- Cooperation in nature
