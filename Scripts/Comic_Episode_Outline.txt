COMIC EPISODE: "THE THIRSTY TREE"
===================================
Format: 8-12 pages, panel-based comic
Status: Concept Phase

LOGLINE
-------
When a young tree can't reach water, Spora learns that the network's
true power is sharing—and that cooperation solves problems no one
can solve alone.

CHARACTERS
----------
- Spora (protagonist, learning)
- Fun-Gus (mentor, guide)
- Thirsty Tree (the problem)
- Background fungi (the community)

PAGE BREAKDOWN
--------------

PAGE 1: THE PROBLEM (3-4 panels)
- Panel 1: Establishing shot of healthy forest section
- Panel 2: Spora traveling through network, happy
- Panel 3: She notices something wrong—a tree's roots look dim
- Panel 4: Close on Spora's concerned face

PAGE 2: INVESTIGATION (3-4 panels)
- Panel 1: Spora approaches the dim roots
- Panel 2: She "listens"—the tree is thirsty
- Panel 3: Tree's message: faint, desperate signal
- Panel 4: Spora: "I have to help!"

PAGE 3: THE ATTEMPT (4 panels)
- Panel 1: Spora tries to push water/nutrients herself
- Panel 2: She strains, glows brighter
- Panel 3: It's not enough—she's too small
- Panel 4: Spora: "I can't do it alone..."

PAGE 4: FINDING FUN-GUS (3-4 panels)
- Panel 1: Spora races through network
- Panel 2: Finds Fun-Gus at a junction node
- Panel 3: Spora explains the problem (speech bubble)
- Panel 4: Fun-Gus smiles knowingly

PAGE 5: THE LESSON (4 panels)
- Panel 1: Fun-Gus: "One thread can't carry enough."
- Panel 2: Fun-Gus: "But what about ALL the threads?"
- Panel 3: Wide shot showing the vast network
- Panel 4: Spora's eyes widen with understanding

PAGE 6: THE CALL (4 panels)
- Panel 1: Fun-Gus sends out a signal—glow pulse
- Panel 2: Other fungi receive it, light up
- Panel 3: Message spreading across network
- Panel 4: "Everyone who can spare a little..."

PAGE 7: THE RESPONSE (splash page or 3 panels)
- Multiple fungi from all directions sending nutrients
- Rivers of glowing resources converging
- Beautiful visual of cooperation in action

PAGE 8: THE SOLUTION (4 panels)
- Panel 1: All the small contributions merge
- Panel 2: They reach the thirsty tree's roots
- Panel 3: The roots brighten, tree receives water
- Panel 4: Above ground—tree's leaves perk up

PAGE 9: CELEBRATION (3-4 panels)
- Panel 1: Network celebrates—pulses of happy color
- Panel 2: Spora beaming
- Panel 3: Fun-Gus: "See? None of us are strong alone."
- Panel 4: Fun-Gus: "But together, we're a forest."

PAGE 10: CLOSING (2-3 panels)
- Panel 1: Spora back at the now-healthy roots
- Panel 2: She sends a small message—"thank you"
- Panel 3: Wide shot, forest glowing with connection
- Final caption: "In Sillium, no one is ever alone."

THEMES
------
- Individual limits vs. collective power
- Asking for help is strength
- Small contributions add up
- Community resilience

VISUAL STYLE NOTES
------------------
- Panel layouts should flow like network paths
- Color temperature: cool when problem, warm when solving
- Glow effects show emotional states
- Underground setting throughout

DIALOGUE PRINCIPLES
-------------------
- Minimal text, visual storytelling primary
- Spora's speech: simple, emotional
- Fun-Gus's speech: wise but not preachy
- Let images carry the message
