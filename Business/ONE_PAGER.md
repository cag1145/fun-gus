# FUN-GUS: The Forest Has WiFi

### A Transmedia Children's Universe Teaching Real Science Through Unforgettable Characters

---

**Genre:** Animated Science-Adventure (Ages 4-9)
**Format:** Animated Series + Picture Books + Digital Content + Merchandise
**Comparable Titles:** Octonauts meets Fantastic Fungi meets Inside Out

---

## THE HOOK

**Nature invented the internet 450 million years before humans.**

Beneath every forest is a living communication network — fungi and trees connected by miles of underground threads, sharing food, water, and warnings. Scientists call it the "Wood Wide Web." We call it **SILLIUM**.

*Fun-Gus* takes children inside this real network through Pixar-quality characters who make mycology, microbiology, and ecology as exciting as any superhero story.

---

## THE STORY

Two curious kids — a boy and a girl — and their teacher discover a glowing fairy ring. They step inside, shrink to spore-size, and enter the living network beneath the forest. Guided by **Fun-Gus** — a warm, funny mushroom mentor — they discover a world of heroes, villains, and alliances spanning fungi, bacteria, and insects.

Every character is based on real science. Every episode teaches something true. And a hidden folklore layer gives adults their own reason to keep watching.
